Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This app is designed with simplicity and safety in mind.

  • My memo does not collect or store any personal data.
  • All notes and content are saved locally on your device only.
  • If you enable Google AdMob, Google may collect limited device data for personalized ads.

You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app at any time.

If you have questions, please contact us at mymemo.support@gmail.com.
